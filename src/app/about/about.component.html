Who Are We?

As the community’s healthcare needs continue to grow, KP Health Clinic supports and maintains a full range of specialty services that serve both inpatients and outpatients as well as their families. We are a medical group specializing in Family Practice, Internal Medicine, Allergic Disorders and Immunology.
We accept many PPO and POS plans, private indemnity insurances and participate in most major IPA’s (Independent Practice Associations) in North Orange County working with health plans such as AETNA, Cigna, Health Net, Blue Shield, TriCare and SCAN to name just a few. We also welcome Medicare, MediCal/CalOptima, Healthy Family, MSI and Cash patients.

Meet KP
